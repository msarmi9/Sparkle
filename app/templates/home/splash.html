Verify Intake Automatically

When your patients use the Sparkle iOS apps, they will get a reminder when it’s time to take their medication. We use sensor data and machine learning to automatically track and verify pill intake, which you’ll be able to see instantly on your online dashboard.

A Fully Integrated Platform

This is a product for doctors that’s as simple as an online log-in, accessible anywhere. You’ll enter prescribed medications for your patients, which will reflect on their iOS mobile apps. Both doctor and patient apps interface using an AWS server that propagates information and intakes instantly back-and-forth.

Keep Patients Accountable

You don’t have to just trust that your patients are taking their medications as prescribed. With Sparkle, you’ll have access and insight like never-before into your patients’ adherence habits. With our online dashboard, you’ll have access to a bird’s-eye view of your patients’ adherence patterns – and be able to quickly spot patients who are deviating from proper treatment.
